05 — Launch Strategy

Phase 1 Days 1–3

Warm-Up

Tease the transformation. Share personal story posts. Ask your audience what they struggle with most. Build curiosity without revealing the product.

Phase 2 Days 4–9

Value Delivery

Drop free value bombs via Stories. Mini workout tips, nutrition hacks, progress frameworks. Position yourself as the expert while building anticipation for what's coming.

Phase 3 Days 10–14

Launch & Sell

Reveal the product. Share testimonials, behind-the-scenes, and urgency-driven CTAs. Use countdown stickers and limited-time bonuses to drive conversions.
